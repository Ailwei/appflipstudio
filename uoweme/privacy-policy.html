U Owe Me Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 23, 2026

AppFlipStudio values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how U Owe Me collects, uses, stores and protects your information when you use the application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use U Owe Me, we may collect:

  • Email address used to create your account.
  • Debt records you create.
  • Payment history.
  • Dates and balances associated with your records.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide the application's features.
  • Synchronize your records across devices.
  • Maintain your account.
  • Improve the reliability and security of the application.

3. Cloud Storage

Your information is securely stored using Firebase cloud services. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with trusted service providers, such as Firebase, when necessary to provide the application's functionality or when required by law.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure or misuse.

6. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.

7. Children's Privacy

U Owe Me is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact AppFlipStudio using the contact information available on our website.
